### 🚀 About Us
We are a small development group providing utilities, academic templates, and automation tools built for the free world. We stand for absolute information freedom, believing that software should be open, accessible, and free for everyone without paying a single dime.

---

### 📦 Key Projects

Here are some of our most active repositories:

*   **📄 [hcmus-unofficial-report-template](https://github.com/khongsomeo/hcmus-unofficial-report-template)**
    *An unofficial LaTeX report template designed for HCMUS students. Ideal for academic assignments, lab reports, and projects.*

*   **🎓 [hcmus-dstn](https://github.com/khongsomeo/hcmus-dstn)**
    *A tool to query student graduation status and details directly using the official HCMUS API.*

*   **📅 [ai-deadlines](https://github.com/khongsomeo/ai-deadlines)**
    *AI and Machine Learning conference deadline countdowns and tracking to keep you ahead of your submissions.*
